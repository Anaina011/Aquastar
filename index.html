We Build Everything As Your Dream

At AQUA STAR TRADING & POOLS SOLUTIONS, we take pride in being a leading provider of high-quality spare parts for swimming pools and water fountains. We also offer construction and maintenance services for indoor and outdoor swimming pools, facilities and pool enclosures, as well as spas, water features, waterfalls, steam rooms, and saunas across Qatar. With years of experience and a deep understanding of the aquatic industry, we are committed to enhancing the functionality and longevity of your water features, ensuring a healthier and happier environment for our clients.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated team of professionals enable us to deliver exceptional products and services tailored to meet the unique needs of schools, hotels, residential villas, apartments, and towers. Our integrated approach encompasses research and development, production, quality control, and training and support, providing a seamless one-stop solution for all your aquatic requirements.

As a responsible and quality supplier of swimming pool spa LED light fittings and spare parts, we source components and products from Spain, the USA, Japan, Taiwan, Korea, India, and China. We partner with various industry-leading manufacturers to bring you the latest in pool technology.
