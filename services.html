AMC Services

At Aqua Star Trading & Pool Solutions, we understand the importance of maintaining the functionality, safety, and aesthetic appeal of your swimming pools, spas, water features, and other aquatic facilities. Our Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) services are designed to provide comprehensive, year-round care to ensure your investments remain in top condition. From routine cleaning and water quality testing to equipment inspections and structural maintenance, we deliver a holistic approach to maintenance that keeps your facilities pristine and operational. Our team uses advanced tools and premium materials to guarantee the highest standards of service, ensuring a hassle-free experience for all our clients.

Our AMC services are tailored to suit the unique requirements of residential, commercial, and hospitality establishments. We provide regular cleaning of pool surfaces, skimming of debris, and chemical balancing to maintain water clarity and hygiene. Equipment such as pumps, filtration systems, and heaters is inspected and serviced regularly to prevent breakdowns and ensure optimal performance. Additionally, we focus on preventive maintenance, identifying potential issues before they escalate into costly repairs. Seasonal preparations, including winterization and summer readiness, are also part of our offerings to ensure your facilities are always ready for use.

In addition to routine maintenance, our AMC plans include quick and efficient repair services to address any unexpected issues. We handle everything from minor leaks and mechanical malfunctions to the replacement of worn-out parts with genuine spare components sourced from global leaders in pool technology. Structural maintenance, such as tile repairs and surface regrouting, is also covered to preserve the visual appeal and integrity of your aquatic facilities. Our commitment to quality and timely service ensures minimal downtime, allowing you to enjoy uninterrupted functionality.

Cleaning

At AQUA STAR TRADING & POOLS SOLUTIONS, we provide expert cleaning services to ensure your swimming pools and water features remain in pristine condition all year round. Proper cleaning is essential to maintaining not just the aesthetic appeal of your aquatic facilities but also their safety and functionality. Our team of experienced professionals uses advanced tools and eco-friendly cleaning agents to deliver top-quality results for residential, commercial, and hospitality clients. Whether it’s a small residential pool or a large commercial installation, we are committed to keeping your water features spotless and inviting.

Our cleaning services include essential tasks such as skimming debris from the water surface, vacuuming pool floors, and scrubbing tiles, walls, and waterlines to prevent stains and scaling. We also focus on maintaining water clarity and hygiene by balancing chemical levels, including pH, chlorine, and alkalinity, using only high-grade cleaning products. For water fountains and decorative water features, we provide thorough cleaning and maintenance, including sediment removal, pump care, and water treatments to keep them operating smoothly and looking beautiful. In addition to routine cleaning, we specialize in deep-cleaning services for pools and water features that need extra care. This includes algae removal, stain treatment, and restoring pools that may have been neglected or are being prepared for a new season. Our experts also tackle issues like hard water deposits and scaling, which can affect the appearance and performance of your water features. No matter the condition or size of the pool, our cleaning solutions are tailored to ensure exceptional results that exceed expectations.

Equipments

DAt AQUA STAR TRADING & POOLS SOLUTIONS, we understand that the performance and longevity of your swimming pool and water feature equipment depend on regular servicing and expert care. Our equipment services are designed to ensure the efficient operation of all components, including pumps, filters, heaters, dosing systems, and lighting. With a team of skilled technicians and access to advanced tools, we provide comprehensive maintenance, repairs, and replacements to keep your systems running smoothly. Whether it’s a residential setup or a commercial facility, we ensure your equipment functions reliably to maintain the quality and safety of your aquatic environment.

Our services include routine inspections and preventive maintenance to identify and address potential issues before they escalate. We thoroughly check for wear and tear, leaks, or malfunctions in critical equipment, such as circulation pumps, filtration systems, and valves. Regular servicing, including lubrication, calibration, and cleaning, ensures that these components operate at peak efficiency while reducing the risk of unexpected breakdowns. By prioritizing preventive care, we help you avoid costly repairs and extend the lifespan of your equipment.

When repairs are necessary, we provide quick and reliable solutions using genuine, high-quality spare parts sourced from trusted global manufacturers. We restore every component to optimal condition, from repairing pool pumps and motors to replacing damaged filters or heaters. Our technicians are trained to handle the latest technologies, including energy-efficient and automated systems, ensuring that your pool equipment is upgraded and maintained to the highest standards. We also provide expert advice on system optimization to enhance performance and reduce energy consumption.

Design

At AQUA STAR TRADING & POOLS SOLUTIONS, we specialize in creating custom-designed swimming pools, water features, and aquatic facilities that reflect your vision while maintaining the highest standards of quality and functionality. Our design services focus on blending innovation with aesthetics to create stunning aquatic spaces for residential, commercial, and hospitality projects. Whether you envision a sleek infinity pool, an intricate fountain, or a large-scale recreational facility, our expert design team is committed to turning your ideas into reality, tailored to suit your space, preferences, and budget.

Our design process begins with a detailed consultation to understand your goals and the unique requirements of your project. Using advanced design tools and 3D modeling technology, we provide a clear visualization of your concept, enabling you to see how the finished project will look in your space. This approach allows you to make informed decisions about every detail, from pool shape and size to finishes, materials, and additional features like waterfalls, jets, or LED lighting. We ensure every element of the design is thoughtfully planned to maximize both beauty and functionality.

Beyond aesthetics, our designs are guided by practicality and durability. We incorporate energy-efficient and environmentally friendly systems into our designs, focusing on sustainability without compromising performance. From choosing the right materials to integrating advanced technologies like smart controls and automated cleaning systems, we ensure your aquatic features are easy to maintain and built to last. Additionally, our designs comply with international safety and quality standards, providing you with peace of mind that your project is secure and reliable.
